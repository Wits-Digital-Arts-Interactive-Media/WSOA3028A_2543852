Blog Page Design

Iteration 1

The blog page will include all my writing. This includes my blogs, essays, and game analyses. They will include links to each of the blog pages. Clicking the link will bring users to another page that just consists of the selected blog.

This page will stand as a navigational check point for all my writing. This will allow users to easily see all my documented writing in a neat and concise manner.

Iteration 2: Bento UI

I decided to put all my writting on one page. This will make navigaiton less complicated as the users doesnt have to click around as much. All they have to do is scroll down and click on the title that interests them! When the player clicks on a title, it expands to reveal the whole writting piece.
