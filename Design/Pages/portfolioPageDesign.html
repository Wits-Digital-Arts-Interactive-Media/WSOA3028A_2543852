Portfolio Page Design

Iteration 1

My portfolio page will include displays of my most recent projects that will display in a scrolling format. They will continuously scroll from right to left and each block will display art or snippets of the games/projects. Links to my itch.io and GitHub accounts will be made available if users would like to see the full extent of my work.

Users will be able to hover over the projects to stop the scrolling and a pop-up window will appear giving more insight into each project. Users will also be able to scroll through the reel whilst their cursor is hovering over the area.

Iteration 2: Bento UI

This iteration is insported by the bento UI trend. It allows me to eligantly display my projects in select boxes. The size and shape of the box and gravitate the users attention to certain projects I want them to focus on.
