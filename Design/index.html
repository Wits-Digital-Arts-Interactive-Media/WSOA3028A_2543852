Goal Alignment

The primary goal of this website is to provide a user-friendly browsing experience while highlighting my work and personality through its design. The website should be responsive and interactive as users navigate through pages and content, offering an engaging experience.

Style Guide

Colour Palette

Since this color palette aims to invoke a feeling of calmness in the user, I decided to incorporate different shades of blue. Blue is a cool colour that is usually associated with calmness.

Aesthetic

The design of the website was inspired by minimalism. Minimalism communicates meaning through its open spaces, so I have decided not to over-decorate the website. I have spaced the elements in an open manner that invites the open space to create a sense of freedom and comfort.

Design Elements

I have animated a blue gradient on certain elements to create areas of interest that garner more eye traffic. This blue gradient is used sparingly to highlight the importance of certain elements. The slow and progressive animation of the gradient creates a sense of serenity and calmness that suits the goal of the website's aesthetic.
